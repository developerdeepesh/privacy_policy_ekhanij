eKhanij — Privacy Policy

eKhanij app is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information you provide voluntarily, including:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Gender
  • State, district and city
  • Profile details submitted by you

1.2 Location Information

We may collect your GPS/location when you use location features (for routing, nearby places, temple navigation, events and services). Saved locations you add are stored to provide app functionality. Location is used only for app features and is not sold to third parties.

1.3 Device & Usage Data

  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • IP address and device identifiers (for analytics & notifications)
  • Usage data such as screens visited and feature usage

1.4 Media & Files

Photos you upload (for example, Lost & Found submissions) are used only for the purpose you choose.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide navigation, maps and routing
  • Show nearby services (medical, police, water points, parking)
  • Display temple and event information
  • Enable Lost & Found submissions and help recover items
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud

We do not sell your personal data to unaffiliated third parties.

3. Permissions We Use

To provide full functionality the app may request:

  • Location — for navigation & nearby services
  • Camera — to capture photos for Lost & Found
  • Storage/Photos — to pick and upload images
  • Notifications — to deliver important alerts
  • Internet — for API calls and content updates

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share limited data with trusted service providers for:

  • Map & routing services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Push notifications and messaging (e.g., Firebase)
  • Cloud storage

Examples of providers: Google Maps / MapMyIndia / OpenStreetMap, Firebase (FCM, Analytics). These providers follow strict privacy and security standards.

We may disclose data if required by law or court order.

5. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted APIs and restricted access. While we strive to keep your data secure, no system is completely immune to risk.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Update your profile information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Disable location via device settings
  • Turn off notifications
  • Uninstall the app to stop data collection

To request data deletion or other privacy inquiries, contact us at: mpssdi@mp.gov.in

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
